About Bryony

Bryony is a successful dressage rider based in the Macedon Ranges in Victoria.

She began her competitive equestrian journey under the guidance of well-known horseman Leigh Dunstan who took her from a novice pony club rider to a Royal Show place getter, and played a crucial in helping source Bryony’s future FEI horse, Batten Dutch Image (Dutch) and starting her successful career in the dressage ring.

In Bryony’s Young Rider years she was a member of both the Victorian and Australian Equestrian Australia Young Rider Squads and has competed successfully in all levels up to FEI.

Bryony has trained under the likes of Victoria Hamilton, Richard Weis and Graham Chapple all of whom have played an important part in making her the rider and trainer she is today.

Since 2005 Bryony has trained and worked closely with her mentor Level 3 EA Coach & A Level Judge, Judy Peel. Through this training and experience with Judy, Bryony has developed a non-confrontational approach to training her horses; paying close attention to how her body influences the horse and giving every horse she rides the chance to process what she is asking before perform every movement with relaxation and willingness.

Services

Bryony has been competing and training horses for over 20 years. Having trained horses from a 4yo to FEI level and competed numerous horses at Royal Shows around the country, she knows what it takes to train a horse through to the highest level.

Specialising in the exercising and training of dressage and show horses, Bryony is fully insured and available to exercise and train horses of all ages/types, however she is not suited to breakers or re-training problem horses.

Casual Training

Bryony is available to travel to your property to exercise/train your horse for short or long term engagements.

Servicing areas: Southern Macedon Ranges (eg. Gisborne, Macedon, Romsey, Woodend), Sunbury, Oaklands and surrounds.

In House Training

Bryony has limited spaced available to take horse in for training at her home base in New Gisborne. Property includes private or shared paddock and all weather dressage arena.

Horses will receive regular training, daily supervision, feeding twice daily or as required. There are a number of young horses on my property so any horse that comes in for training must be sensible and good to handle.

Competitions

In addition to either of the above services Bryony is also available to campaign your horse in either the dressage or show ring.

Bryony regularly attends competitions and is available for full competition services including show prep if required.

Please contact Bryony for further information and rates on any of the above.

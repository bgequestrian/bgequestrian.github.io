Bryony Giusti

Dressage Competitor and Trainer

About Bryony

Bryony has been competing and training horses for over 20 years. Having trained horses from 4yo to FEI level and competed numerous horses at Royal Shows around the country, she knows what it takes to train a horse through to the highest level.

Bryony has developed a non-confrontational approach to training horses; paying close attention to how her body influences the horse and giving every horse she rides the chance to process what she is asking before performing every movement with relaxation and willingness.

Services

Specialising in the exercising and training of dressage and show horses, Bryony is fully insured and available to exercise and train horses of all ages/types, however she is not suited to breakers or re-training problem horses.

Casual Training

Bryony is available to travel to your property to exercise/train your horse for short or long term engagements.

Servicing areas: Southern Macedon Ranges (eg. Gisborne, Macedon, Romsey, Woodend), Sunbury, Oaklands and surrounds.

In House Training

Bryony has limited spaces available to take horse in for training at her home base in New Gisborne.

Competitions

Bryony regularly attends shows and competitions and is available to campaign your horse at one-off events or for a full season.
